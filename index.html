Our Story

Coffee is our love language.

Grounds & Grace was born in 2018 from a simple obsession: the perfect cup. We started as a tiny corner spot with a secondhand espresso machine and a belief that coffee should feel like a warm embrace, not just a morning chore.

Every bean we source travels a story. From the misty highlands of Ethiopia to the volcanic soils of Guatemala, we work directly with farmers who share our reverence for the craft. No shortcuts. No compromises.

We've grown, but we've never lost what matters: the smell of a fresh roast, the sound of steam, and a place where you're always welcome to stay a little longer.
